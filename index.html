About the Game

Our Adana is a tribute to the golden era of arcade fighting games. With meticulously crafted animations and smooth, fluid gameplay, we bring the classic experience to your modern device. Choose from a range of unique characters, each with their own special abilities, fighting styles, and backstory. Engage in epic one-on-one battles against a variety of AI opponents or challenge your friends in multiplayer mode for ultimate bragging rights. Whether you're a seasoned fighting game veteran or a newcomer looking for exciting action, this game is designed to deliver hours of fun and competitive gameplay.

Features

High-quality 2D Animations: Every fight comes to life with smooth, fluid animations that give your character's moves a realistic and powerful feel. The attention to detail ensures that every punch, kick, and special move looks as exciting as it feels. Multiple Characters with Unique Abilities: Choose from a wide roster of fighters, each with their own distinctive fighting styles and special abilities. From lightning-fast martial artists to powerful brawlers, there's a character for every playstyle. Dynamic and Challenging Levels: Fight in a variety of environments, each with their own set of challenges and obstacles. Whether you're battling on a city rooftop, in a futuristic arena, or in a mysterious jungle, the surroundings play an important role in shaping the combat.
